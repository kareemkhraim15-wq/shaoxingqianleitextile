Two Decades of Milling

Built on the loom.

Founded in the heart of Keqiao — Shaoxing's China Textile City — SHAOXING QIANLEI TEXTILE CO has spent over twenty years turning raw yarn into fabric that holds its shape, its color, and its promise.

Our mills run cotton, silk, linen, wool, and technical blends through the same discipline: consistent GSM, accurate color-matching, and finishing that survives the wash test as well as the runway. Today we supply cutting studios, garment factories, and independent designers across more than sixty countries.
